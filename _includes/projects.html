Bio

Aliyah is an undergraduate student at National Taiwan University, double majoring in Electrical Engineering and Economics.

Her research interests lie in speech, natural language processing and machine learning. She has worked with Prof. Lin-Shan Lee and Prof. Hung-Yi Lee on personalized dialogue response generation. She is also currenting working with Prof. Yu-Chiang Frank Wang on stochastic and controllable image-to-video generation.

When she is not coding, she likes to read, take film photos and mentor younger students in the fields of STEM.
