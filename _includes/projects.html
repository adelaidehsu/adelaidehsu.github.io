About Me

I am a PhD student in EECS at UC Berkeley, working with Bin Yu and affiliated with BAIR. I am funded by a 2-year Chancellor Fellowship. My research interests include statistical machine learning and causal inference.
I graduated from National Taiwan University in 2020, where I studied electrical engineering and economics. I had the pleasure of working with professors Lin-Shan Lee, Hung-Yi Lee and Yu-Chiang Frank Wang on research topics in natural language processing and computer vision. When I am not coding, I enjoy reading, being an amateur film photographer, and mentoring younger students in the fields of STEM.
